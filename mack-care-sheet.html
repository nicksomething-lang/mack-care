Walks & parks
On leash
He pulls a little. Treats help. There are some barky dogs on the route — they're harmless, but Mack may want to cross the street. He'll follow your lead.
After every walk
Check his paws for thorns/burrs and refresh his water. He'll usually lick out any burrs himself — totally normal.

Mack 101
Arrival
He'll bark when he hears you — he'll stop once you're inside. He may then zoom around the garden boxes. Pets and scratches are his love language, say hi there.
Calming him down
Use a calm, gentle tone and tell him to go to his bed. Give him belly rubs once he's there. That usually does it.
When you're leaving
He may hide under the bed in the bedroom — totally okay, he just knows. Just do a quick paw check for thorns before you head out.
Normal behavior
  • Zoom energy, then deep naps — totally normal Aussiedoodle
  • If he's licking his paws after a walk, he's telling you something's stuck — check and help remove any burrs (pull them out or brush them off)
  • Loves the garden area for a quick run
  • Gentle, friendly with people
